My name is Nikki. I am 31 years old and I grew up in Orem, Utah. I currently live in Saratoga Springs, Utah. My husband and I have been married for 8 years. We have 3 mini pigs: Lilly, Booker, and Piper. We spoil them like children!

I love educating people about mini pigs. There is a lot of misinformation out there and correcting it is a big undertaking. My husband and I created and maintain a website called www.pigipedia.org. It is a public wiki that anyone can use to find general information and health conditions for mini pigs. I also administrate a Facebook group by the same name. The site and the group allow anyone to ask questions and receive answers knowleable admins as well as current mini pig owners and experts.
